My mission and ways of working

Hi there! Thank you for taking the time to browse through my website. My mission is to make organizations a lot better at marketing communications. I am pursuing this goal by offering myself to be part of your marketing team for a period of time. I will listen to the aspirations of your marketing management team, figure out possibilities for improved efficiencies and impact and then start the hard work of delivering on the agreed targets as part of your team. I believe in transparency, fair and square co-operation and extremely detailed business focus as the key facets to any successful change program. I think the marketing team should work smart so that the
marketing budget can work hard to deliver on the targets and plans. Further, the required skills to deliver change should be available to most organizations, not just the big and mighty. Therefore I have also started building a network of highly experienced and like-minded freelance consultants that I can draw upon, if need be. I know that I don’t know everything, but I know who does and can help tailor a team that will be best suited for your interim needs. Let’s have a chat over a cuppa or over Teams and see how I could work with you for better business results!
Yours, Janne

About me

Janne Lehtinen Your Interim CMO

I am a very broadly experienced, innovative and customer-oriented marketing and business development professional with a proven track record of turning market insights to executable services that produce competitive advantage.

I take pleasure in tackling a problem, large or small, analyzing it with the team, and finding different solutions that can be deployed and that will deliver the intended results. I am very direct and business oriented, but I can also get very excited and inspiring when I identify new opportunities for growth for my customers. I think business creativity is one of my biggest assets. 

I am also very passionate about targets, metrics, ROI and effectiveness. (My absolute favorite advertising beauty contest is EFFIE.) Setting the right targets and resourcing against them is not something teams do every day. That is why it can seem hard, but great target setting one of the key criteria for success in any assignment – or for any teams working together. That is why I think any marketer that has a substantive annual spend, should take a keen ook at their effectiveness continuously, from multiple angles and theory perspectives.
